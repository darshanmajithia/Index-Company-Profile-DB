History & Milestones

28
1928 — The Origin
Late Sheikh Shafei Al Madani starts a tailoring & textile trading business in Deira Old Souq — then the largest market in the region. The family legacy begins.
83
1983 — Trading Arm
Dr. Abdulsalam Al Madani establishes INDEX Trading & Investment (ITI), expanding from textiles into project management, investment, and general trading.
91
1991 — Events Pioneer
INDEX Conferences & Exhibitions (ICE) is founded. Starts hosting events in small rooms — grows to become the UAE's leading events company. Launches AEEDC Dubai, now the world's largest dental conference.
05
2005 — Healthcare
INDEX Healthcare Management (IHCM) established as a health consultancy serving UAE, Gulf, and MENA region.
06
2006 — Holding Formed
INDEX Holding formally established as the parent company, bringing all businesses under one corporate umbrella. Media, Design, and Medical Center subsidiaries added.
18
2018+ — Global Expansion
Opens offices in South Korea and Singapore. Launches INDEX IPC (Integrated Platform Company) in Riyadh, KSA. Enters coffee/F&B through DXBlends brand. Expands to 25+ annual events globally.

Finance Manager — Role Context

What the Finance Manager Will Oversee

Multi-Entity Consolidation
10+ subsidiaries across events, healthcare, trading, media, textile, and international offices. Group consolidation with intercompany eliminations across UAE, KSA, Korea, Singapore.
Revenue Model Complexity
Events revenue is project-based and seasonal (25+ events/year). Trading revenue is brand-dependent (F&B, retail). Healthcare is recurring/consultancy. Textile is steady retail. Requires event-level P&L tracking.
Regulatory Landscape
UAE VAT, UAE Corporate Tax (new), Free Zone compliance (FZ LLC), KSA tax regulations for IPC Riyadh entity. Multiple jurisdictions = multiple filing calendars.
Strategic Growth Phase
Active international expansion (KSA, APAC). New brand launches (DXBlends coffee). Feasibility studies for new ventures, capex evaluation, and investment decisions needed from finance.
Stakeholder Ecosystem
Family-led business — Chairman, Group CEO, and subsidiary heads are all Al Madani family. Finance Manager must build trust with family leadership while maintaining institutional rigour.
Risk & Controls
Event-based businesses carry concentration risk (large events = large revenue swings). Cash flow timing around event cycles. Need robust internal controls across diverse business units.
